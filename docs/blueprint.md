# **App Name**: DMG Stream

## Core Features:

- Responsive Global Navigation: Implement a fixed header with the DMG Records logo, primary navigation links (Home, The Artist, Releases, Productions, About, Contact), and a responsive mobile menu toggle.
- Hero Showcase Section: Create an engaging landing section featuring the 'DMG Records: Produtora e Gravadora Oficial do Cantor Vini Amaral' title and subtitle, alongside prominent call-to-action buttons ('Ouvir agora', 'Ver lançamentos', 'Entrar em contato') and a dynamic background image relevant to music or studio settings.
- Vini Amaral Artist Profile: Dedicated content section showcasing Vini Amaral's photo, comprehensive biography, musical style, and highlights of his career, providing fans with detailed insights.
- Dynamic Music Releases Gallery: A responsive grid display for album and single artworks, including track titles, integrated audio player functionality for previews (via embeds), and direct links to popular music streaming platforms (Spotify, YouTube, etc.).
- DMG Records Services Overview: Clearly present the range of professional services offered by DMG Records, such as music recording, production, mixing, mastering, and creation of exclusive beats.
- AI-Powered Release Copy Assistant: An internal tool to assist DMG Records staff in quickly generating compelling marketing blurbs and social media captions for new music releases, suggesting promotional text based on key track details, genre, and artist mood.
- Comprehensive Contact & Social Hub: Provide a secure and user-friendly contact form for inquiries, complemented by clearly displayed professional email address, WhatsApp contact, and social media links for various platforms.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Scheme: Dark mode for a sleek, modern, and professional aesthetic, heavily using black as the primary background.
- Primary Color: Red (#FF0000) for highlighting key interactive elements, calls-to-action, and impactful statements to convey energy and passion.
- Background Color: Deep Black (#000000) providing a sophisticated and immersive backdrop for all content, echoing studio and urban music vibes.
- Accent Color: Neon Green (#00FF00) for subtle details, special effects, and emphasis on critical information or stylistic flourishes.
- Text & Secondary Elements: White (#FFFFFF) for body text, navigation labels, and minimalist UI components, ensuring high contrast against the dark background.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable design, fitting both large titles and smaller paragraphs in a minimalist, professional context.
- Icons should be vector-based, clean, and minimalist, utilizing solid fills or sharp line-art styles. Focus on icons relevant to music playback (play, pause, next), social media, and communication.
- Utilize a clean, organized, and responsive layout with generous negative space to enhance content readability and maintain a professional feel. Content sections should be full-width with a mobile-first design approach.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth animations for hover effects on buttons, links, and album covers, as well as gentle transitions for page scrolls or menu openings, contributing to a premium user experience.
